My passion

The topic of this website is "Young in Canberra". Canberra is suitable for Young people and you won't feel boring to travel here!

You can:
  • Enjoying the mysterious Aurora in the peak of black mountain
  • Tasting fantastic dishes from all over the world in Light festival
  • Joining in the Southern Hemisphere biggest flower show
  • Go skiing with several friends in the natual mountain
  • Watching an openair film facing Griffin Lake and back to Parliment house
Since all the great sightseeings can be found from wikipedia, instegram or other travel agencies and all the routes and scheduals are similar, it's less meaningful to include same contents. What's more, I heard many people complaint that Canberra has nothing special, I hope all and keep updating activities in my web page may give them a new understanding about this flower city -- Canberra.
